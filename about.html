ABOUT US


posted on
August 20, 2022

Manhwa Zown is a place created by the fans, Created for the fans. It's a place where you can find high-quality manga online absolutely free. This website has been made specifically for manga lovers who want to read all kinds of manga, manhwa, and even manhua.

Team Manhwa Zown aims ot provide great quality manga related content to of people of all agaes. The idea is to spread love for manga and share it with people all around the world. We believe that the amazing stories in different manga need to be shared with the world. With that in mind, we created Manhwa Zown and decided to make it free tfor everyone.

There's something very unique about manga, it's a great blend of art and text. Its exquisite story style makes it the perfect way to portray lives of different people as well as characters. But many people are unable to read manga becausse they don't want to buy it, they want to be able to read it online for free and there's where Manhwa Zown comes in. Our idea is exactly to gives the fans what they want. We want to build a platform that anyone can axxess and read quality manga.

We believe that Manga is more that just comic. It's a medium of expression and over time, it has become an art. The idea that one mangaka can inspire millions of people around the world feels almost magical. We at Manhwa Zown want to help the people and spread their message. Manhwa Zown is our little effort to make the manga and anime community a little more accessible. We believe in the freedom of reading manga and that drives us to pursue our goal of spreading the love form manga in the world.

Our Reason for creating Manhwa Zown:

We have made the website in a way that it's very easy to use. We made all of the sections as simple as possible. You can bookmark any chapter you want and read it later by signing up. You can also, search up any manga you want easily using the search bar. If you want us to add any manga/manhwa/manhua that we haven't already added then just email us at manhwazown@gmail.com
